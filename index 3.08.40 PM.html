What's New

  • Dublin, CA – May 6, 2021 – After an extensive search process, the Board of Trustees of the Dublin Unified School District has selected Chris Funk as the new superintendent for the district. Currently the superintendent of the East Side Union High School District, which administers 16 high schools with a combined enrollment of approximately 26,500 students in Santa Clara County, Mr. Funk brings significant leadership experience at the district level. Pending contract negotiations and a background check, Mr. Funk’s appointment will be confirmed at the May 11, 2021 Board meeting and he will begin as the district’s new superintendent on July 1, 2021.
